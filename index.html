Who Are We.

Vision

At Western Wonders Immigration, our goal is to understand your Canadian immigration needs and provide guidance and solutions to make your journey successful. We believe in the following principles while serving you:

  • Provide professional quality of service that exceeds your expectations.
  • Support your immigration journey from start to finish.
  • Offer fair and valuable service at a price that meets your budget.
  • Conduct business in ethical manner and in compliance with the Code of Professional Ethics and all relevant regulations as outlined by ICCRC.

Consultant

Vishvek Babbar is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) registered with the ICCRC (No. R708805). Being an immigrant himself, Vishvek and his team understand the importance and success of each immigration application.

Vishvek and his team have extensive knowledge and experience of Canadian Immigration laws and regulations. The team is well informed of the constantly changing Canadian Immigration landscape and policies and can provide you with the best possible solution for your needs.
